# Botanical Garden

A fresh and organic theme featuring vibrant garden-inspired colors for lively presentations.

## Color Palette

- **Fern Green**: `#4a7c59` — Rich natural green
- **Marigold**: `#f9a620` — Bright floral accent
- **Terracotta**: `#b7472a` — Earthy warm tone
- **Cream**: `#f5f3ed` — Soft neutral backgrounds

## Typography

- **Headers**: DejaVu Serif Bold
- **Body Text**: DejaVu Sans

## Best Used For

Garden centers, food presentations, farm-to-table content, botanical brands, natural products.

## Divergence Notes (Ayurvedic Retreat Platform)

Botanical Garden's marigold (`#f9a620`) is the closest preset to saffron gold — the Ayurvedic CTA colour. However, it is too saturated and bright for a trust-first platform. Desaturate toward `#C4892A` to read as warm authority rather than marketplace energy.

The terracotta (`#b7472a`) is well-positioned as a mid-tone surface colour. Consider it for section background alternation rather than as a dominant colour.

Fern green is softer than Forest Canopy's deep green — use Forest Canopy's green for the one dark section; Botanical's fern for decorative details.
