# Desert Rose

A soft and sophisticated theme with dusty, muted tones perfect for elegant presentations.

## Color Palette

- **Dusty Rose**: `#d4a5a5` — Soft primary color
- **Clay**: `#b87d6d` — Earthy accent
- **Sand**: `#e8d5c4` — Warm neutral backgrounds
- **Deep Burgundy**: `#5d2e46` — Rich dark contrast

## Typography

- **Headers**: FreeSans Bold
- **Body Text**: FreeSans

## Best Used For

Fashion presentations, beauty brands, wedding planning, interior design, boutique businesses.

## Divergence Notes (Ayurvedic Retreat Platform)

Desert Rose is too feminine and boutique for the retreat aggregation platform — it reads as a spa brand rather than a trusted health authority. However:

- The sand background (`#e8d5c4`) is close to the warm parchment tone needed — slightly cooler than ideal.
- The clay (`#b87d6d`) could function as a terracotta mid-tone for section backgrounds.
- Deep burgundy (`#5d2e46`) is too fashion-adjacent for an Ayurvedic trust signal.

Use Desert Rose as a reference for the sandy parchment background register only. Discard the rose and burgundy in favour of Forest Canopy's deep green and a saffron gold CTA.
